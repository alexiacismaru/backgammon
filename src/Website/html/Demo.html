The aim of the game is to move all of your pawns to your side of the board and remove all of you opponent's pawns from and table.
This game is inspired by the ancient game of BACKGAMMON, which is a two-player game where each player has fifteen pieces that move
between twenty-four triangles according to the roll of two dice. Backgammon involves a combination of strategy and luck. With each
roll of the dice, players must choose from numerous options for moving their pieces and anticipate possible counter-moves by the opponent.

1. As soon as you open your game you will be asked to
enter a username:

After you're done, press ENTER for the next page!

2. Here you'll see a brief description of the game:

Press the N key to see the rules!

3. Make sure you read the rules before playing:

Press the N key to start the game!

4. To decide who's turn is first you need to roll the dice:

Press ENTER to roll the dice!

5. If you got the bigger number then it's your turn:

Firstly enter the number of the row from which you want to
remove the pawn from and then to row you want to move it to!

6. Wait for the PC's turn:

Go back and forth until you or the PC wins the game!
